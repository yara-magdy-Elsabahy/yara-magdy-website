Siwa oasis

After a long drive through the barren landscape of the Western Desert, you won’t believe your eyes when you reach Siwa for the first time.
You will see a beautiful island full of mineral springs, salt lakes and endless Olive and Palm groves. Finish your day by taking a dip at Cleopatra's Pool, where the legendary queen herself is believed to have once swum.
Don’t forget to buy some Siwan Crafts on your way, and visit the charming island " Taghaghien". Adventurous travelers will also get their fix of fun and excitement by heading for a safari into the great sand sea or trying out quad biking in the Western Desert of Egypt. It is advisable to visit Siwa and any other Western Desert destination in the cooler months of the year, from October to April . as temperatures in summer can reach up to 35-45 C.
